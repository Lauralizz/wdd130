Overview

Purpose

Our purpose at Norman Rafting is to provide memorable experiences for families and friends. Thrill, excitement, and surprise are guaranteed with the many twists and turns our trails provide. The beautiful scenery also highlights the memorable experience we strive to present.

Audience

Our target audience is parents traveling with their families looking for memorable experiences. Age: 25-50. This site will provide them with info on location, pricing, and images to look forward to their experience. This site will not include a video yet. They will access my website on mostly computer, but also mobile device.
